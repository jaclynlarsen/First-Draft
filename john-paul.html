JOHN PAUL

What was your childhood dream job?

"An astronaut."

What started this dream or who influenced it?

My cousin worked for NASA and I remember thinking how cool that was. Visiting the space center was always so much fun and I think a younger me thought that it would be cool to be paid to go to the moon.

How has this dream for your future changed or has it remained the same?

"Well, I'm currently working towards becoming a CPA."

Well, I'm currently working towards becoming a CPA. So that is pretty different than my original astronaut dreams.

What have you done to achieve this dream?

For my new dream job, I have taken several courses in college that will help me achieve this. I have been completing as many internships as possible and studying a lot in order to be prepared for the CPA exams.

How close are you to achieving this dream or has it formed a new one?

"Within two years..."

Within two years I will be able to sit for the CPA exams. Once I pass those, if all goes well, then I will officially be a licensed CPA.
