GRACE

What was your childhood dream job?

"A veterinarian."

What started this dream or who influenced it?

I feel like a lot of young kids say they want to be vets at some point in their life. Personally, I loved animals and adored always having cats as pets, so I that's where it stemmed from.

How has this dream for your future changed or has it remained the same?

"My absolute dream would be to work in sports medicine/orthopedics."

This dream has definitely changed for me; although I remain in medicine I want to become a Physician's Assistant (PA) instead. My absolute dream job would be to specialize within the profession and work in sports medicine/orthopedics.

What have you done to achieve this dream?

"I've worked really hard in school, have shadowed several PAs..."

To become a PA, I've worked really hard in school, have shadowed several PAs, and have worked at a nursing home to gain patient care hours. I always temporarily worked at a PA school to make valuable connections and learn more about the program itself at JWU.

How close are you to achieving this dream or has it formed a new one?

"...anywhere from 3-4 years away."

Depends on how you're looking at it I guess, but I'm anywhere from 3-4 years away (working after undergrad, getting in and going to PA school, and then graduating) from becoming and working as a PA. I'd like to think that's not too far away!
