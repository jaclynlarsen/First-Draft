LAUREN

What was your childhood dream job?

"A professional soccer player."

What started this dream or who influenced it?

My parents played soccer so they had me playing from a young age as well. This is what influenced this dream.

How has this dream for your future changed or has it remained the same?

"Now I am working to become an engineer."

This isn't my dream job any longer as I just want to be involved in the sport for fun rather than try to make a career out of it. Now I am working to become an engineer. My love for cars and learning from my dad who is also an engineer has influenced me to want to follow in his steps.

What have you done to achieve this dream?

I am currently majoring in engineering in college. I have always helped my dad with any of his projects so that I can try to learn as much as possible through experience.

How close are you to achieving this dream or has it formed a new one?

I am still quite a bit away from achieving this dream job. I transferred schools after freshmen year and that has postponed my progress a bit since a lot of my courses did not transfer cleanly.
