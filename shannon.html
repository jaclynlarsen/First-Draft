SHANNON

What was your childhood dream job?

"A chef."

What started this dream or who influenced it?

I can't really recall who or what might've influenced this. I think just growing up cooking always seemed fun. I also was never really a picky eater as a kid so maybe it was just the fact that I liked food.

How has this dream for your future changed or has it remained the same?

"Now I would like to be a sports dietician."

I no longer want to be a chef. Now I would like to be a sports dietician. It's great because it combines my love of sports and passion for clean eating into one career.

What have you done to achieve this dream?

"Going to college helped me figure this career change out."

How close are you to achieving this dream or has it formed a new one?

As I mentioned, my childhood dream job has changed. I hope to be a sports dietician in about 3 years.
